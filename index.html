Facts

Tearing apart string cheese actually affects its flavor perception. When a string of cheese is ripped, it exposes more contact with saliva, releasing extra flavors.

Nutrition

One string cheese contains just over 6 grams of dairy protein and 85 calories. It is an excellent source of vitamins and minerals including calcium, vitamin B12, and selenium. It contains 5.8 grams of fats, and and 1.3 grams of carbohydrates, 0 grams of fibre, and less than 1 gram of sugar.
